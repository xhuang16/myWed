My current and past research

We Got the Collected cells Out

We have been trouble shooting the collection system for our prior device. Now we are able to collect enough cells to form large pellet after spinning. Next step, impedance or flow cytometry!

Collection Channels in New DEP Device

We designed and integrated the next iteration expansion collection channels to our previous orifice DEP design. The expansion section is designed to amplify separation distance between streamlines. Here we demonstrate collection of no DEP and DEP fractionated RBCs.

COMSOL Simulation

Since the lab locked down due to COVID, I have spent my time learning COMSOL. The goal I have in mind it to use COMSOL to better design electrodes to maximize for the nDEP and the pDEP case.

First DEP in new device

We designed and fabricated a dynamic DEP device. The video here shows the cells being deflected under nDEP. The expansion of the cells also displays the spatial extend of the electric field.

RBC Pearl Chaining

After spending months of troubleshooting the fabrication of these microfluidic devices, I am finally able to move on to the experimental stage. Here we show field penetration across the microchannel by demonstrating pearl chaining of RBCs.

Embossing Microfluidic Devices in COC

I have been assigned to develop a process to fabricate high aspect ratio cell separating microfluidic device in COC through a hot embossing process. The project is done for CytoRecovery Inc.
